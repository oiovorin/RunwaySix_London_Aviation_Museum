About the museum

The London Aviation Museum preserves and shares the aviation history of London, Ontario. Closely connected to the legacy of RCAF Station Crumlin and the historic Airman's Canteen, the museum highlights the city's role in the British Commonwealth Air Training Plan and honours local Royal Canadian Air Force members who served during the Second World War, ensuring their contributions are remembered for future generations.
