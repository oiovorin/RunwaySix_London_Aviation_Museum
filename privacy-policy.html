Privacy Policy

The London Aviation Museum is committed to protect privacy policy of the visitors. We value your trust and ensure your information(s) are handled securely.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we may collect when you visit our website, how that information is used, and the precautions measure we use to secure it.

Information we collect

We may collect some of your personal information while you interact with our website. That includes:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Phone Number

Use of Information

The collected information may be used to:

  • Response to messages
  • Provide Information about museum
  • Maintain website security

We do not misuse or trade your personal information to third parties.

Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect personal information from loss, misuse, or unauthorized access. Access to personal data is limited to authorized personnel only.

Retention of Information

Personal information will only be retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law..

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page.
