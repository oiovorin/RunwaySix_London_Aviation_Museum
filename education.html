Educational Resources

Explore, Learn, Take Flight

Interactive lessons, downloadable resources, and virtual experiences designed for teachers and students.

Our educational programming is thoughtfully designed to support Ontario curriculum outcomes in History, Science, and STEM learning. The London Aviation Museum provides teachers with ready-to-use lesson plans, printable worksheets, discussion prompts, and virtual resources that make it easy to integrate aviation history into classroom instruction.

Each resource encourages inquiry-based learning, critical thinking, and real-world application. Whether teaching the evolution of flight technology or exploring the impact of aviation on society, educators can rely on our materials to deliver meaningful and engaging learning experiences.

Primary : Students aged 10-18 (grades 5-12)
Secondary : Teachers and homeschool educators looking for historical content and STEM integration

Classroom Sessions

The London Aviation Museum offers engaging classroom sessions designed to connect students directly with aviation history. Whether delivered virtually or in person, each session is interactive, curriculum-aligned, and tailored to your grade level.
Led by knowledgeable presenters, students will explore historic aircraft, uncover the stories behind artifacts, and learn how aviation has shaped the world we live in today.
